Biography

I was born on October 29, 2002 in the city of Kokshetau. We lived there for a short time and by the age of 4 moved to Astana closer to my grandmother. We first lived in another rented apartment until we moved to her house and began to live with her. It was in that house that I spent my entire childhood.

School years

Until I was 5 and sent to elementary school. There I learned some basic languages, which helped a lot in school. After finishing elementary school, I became a first-grader at secondary school No. 8. I studied in the Kazakh class and from the first grade I tried very hard and studied perfectly. Learning at school was easy for me. I participated in various interscholastic Olympiads in different subjects. That's how my school years went.

University years

I graduated from school with a gold medal and passed the UNT with 117 points, which was a good indicator of my knowledge. Here I found a lot of new friends who have common interests with me.
